Immersive Installation

A sensory experience in darkness — visitors feel their way through a tactile maze guided by touch and sound, until the path opens into an encounter with Omu, a living, mythical animatronic presence that answers touch with warmth.

DURATIONMay 2024 – May 2025
TEAM SIZE22
LENGTH10–15 min
FORM15 × 15 ft² interactive maze with an animatronic creature

01

Overview

InSync invites visitors into complete darkness, where every gesture — walking, crawling, sitting, stretching — becomes a way of sensing. Pressure sensors trigger audio cues that guide the journey through shifting textures of moss, leaves, and metal.

02

Experience Principles

01

Embrace the Unknown

Darkness becomes a space for curiosity, inviting players to lean in rather than retreat.

02

Living Interaction

Omu is treated as a living presence rather than an obstacle, responding gently to movement, touch, and proximity.

03

Silent Understanding

Players communicate through movement, rhythm, and proximity, forming connections that extend beyond language.

Refining the creature's proportions and translating gestures of connection into its anatomy.

03 — Final

04

Fabrication Process

Translating Omu into a full-scale animatronic required the visual design, internal structure, movement systems, foam body, and fur surface to work as one approachable physical presence.

Face Construction

A 3D-printed skull formed the base while hand-shaped fur, painted teeth, soft fabric layers, wooden supports, and mechanical joints created Omu's expression and natural head movement.

Tail Mechanism

A flexible fabric surface wraps a 3D-printed skeleton. Internal fishing lines control direction, producing biomimetic movement inspired by fish tails and tentacles.

Body & Breathing

Soft foam pads the body, with thicker edges protecting the wooden frame and a thinner center allowing an internal air pump to expand the surface. This breathing motion reinforces Omu's warm, approachable presence.

05

Learning Through Touch

Without sight, players naturally slowed down, listened more closely, and used both hands to read Omu's scale and expression. Their hesitation giving way to curiosity confirmed that touch could carry the intended emotional arc.

OBSERVATIONPlayers approached cautiously, then shifted into broader and more confident gestures as Omu became familiar.

06   STORYBOARD DEVELOPMENT
From Fear to Trust

01 — Narrative Foundation

The first pass maps a journey from losing sight to discovering Omu through touch, then carrying that connection back into the world.

02 — Interaction & Atmosphere

I clarified Omu's call, the entwining hand gesture, and the blue-violet language that distinguishes the cave encounter.

03 — Final Emotional Rhythm

The final pass strengthens contrast and panel progression so the encounter lands as the emotional center of the experience.

07

Marketing Art

As Art Lead, I used targeted revisions to strengthen hierarchy, readability, atmosphere, and consistency across InSync's promotional artwork.

Judgement Tarot Card

The card evolved toward a clearer interaction between Omu and the player, stronger focal lighting, and a print-ready visual hierarchy.

08

A Quiet Moment with Omu

After months of sketching, storyboarding, building, and testing, Omu no longer felt like an object assembled from foam and fur. These small moments are my personal record of the distance between the first drawing and the presence we finally brought into the room.

For me, the most meaningful part of InSync was watching something imagined on paper become a presence that people could meet, trust, and remember.
